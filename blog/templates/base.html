Shooting Style

My shooting style is different than most in the sense of manipulating ambient light without the use of any built-in or external flash or strobes. I prefer naturally filtering my light through my lens to keep my work as natural and organic as possible and focus on soft surface editing techniques that enhance the picture not alter it or deflect from what has already been captured with over editing and airbrushing. I also specialize in shooting in a monochrome format that I use to exude the natural emotion of the subject.
