Taylor

As I write this, it brings up a lot of memories of who I am and where it all started. This also makes me realize, if you have a dream, don't stop pursuing it! You don't stop pursuing what you're doing until you've reached your definition of success. I started modeling in high school, since I live in a small-big town I used the only resource that I knew, Facebook. I looked high and low to find local photographers and model gigs. After getting into modeling I found out more about myself, and my passion, art. Modeling to me was so much more than being the next best “it” girl. I fell in love with creating an emotion in such a simple, or dramatic way. You become everyone's personal vision without even realizing it, or knowing. Today the modeling industry is changing, and may that be for the better! I believe modeling is just like art, you (model) are the canvas and the painters (stylist) create an image of all the supplies (fashion designers) given to them. Thought modeling I have learned to take things as they came, don't pass up anything that doesn't suit the direction of which you're going. At the end of the day being in fashion has shown me what I'm supposed to do in this thing we call, life. I'm now pursuing a degree in business, and fashion design. I have set up and opened a Boutique in San Marcos, TX, walked in a couple fashions ( even though I 5’5!!), and met so many amazing people. One day I would like to open my own clothing line but on a small scale, I believe having a secret clothing line for the people that really try to discover fashion designers deserve it all.
Much love, and passion!
-Taylor M. Barrier

Mia

Mia Isabella is a print and runway model, actress, singer, entrepreneur, and author. Mia has been modeling for three years and is signed with Nelson Fashion Group, in San Antonio, Texas. She has walked in multiple fashion shows in South Texas, and has appeared in commercials, print advertisements, and recently wrapped her first documentary film. She has been published numerous times and was named Model of the Month by SA Monthly Magazine in October 2016. Last year she was awarded a scholarship to the New York Film Academy. Mia often works as a hostess for networking and marketing events, and at high-profile private functions. Mia’s fashion articles have appeared in Texas magazines, and she plans to release her first book later this year. When Mia is not working, she enjoys traveling and expressing her artistic side through painting and drawing. Agent: Lari Nelson, Nelson Fashion Group
